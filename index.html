We Are The League of Vikings

The League of Vikings is Western Washington University's official League of Legends club. Members are invited to join our Discord and Facebook to ask questions, organize fun events, and keep our community connected. If you're looking to play with other club members, hop onto our Discord server and see who's down to party up. We're a friendly bunch, but if someone is misbehaving let an officer know.

Meetings & Events

Official club games are played bi-weekly on Tuesday nights at 6 PM in CF 26.

Club officers will organize various events per quarter but we also encourage club members to organize events as well.
